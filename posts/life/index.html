Life is like a staircase. You just gotta keep climbing. Otherwise, if you start to overthink it, you’ll have trouble taking the next step.

I think I gotta keep working, at one point I knew a person who was escaping for his life by going to sleep, sleeping was like drugs to her, something keeps you away from facing a lot of things. I don’t like drugs, I don’t like sleep, I don’t like having to face things. I do the same mistake every time. I’ll stop. and I’ll just keep working.
