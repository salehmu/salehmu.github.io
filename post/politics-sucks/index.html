Politics Simply Sucks

categories: Blogs

tags: Politics

Description/Summary

Leaving social media and politics for [couple of years/good].

Content

Politics, political news and anything related to the so-called new world (supposed to be the better one) simply sucks especially if you understand what's going on. Actually it only sucks if you understand what's going on, for instance if you're reading that the U.S. bombarded the Red Cross headquarters in Afghanistan or Aljazira headquarters because they're position for Al-Qaeda terrorists, well that's really a good news, Al Jazeera should only blame themselves.

But if you're understanding what was really going on, you will understand that what was happening is an endeavor to restrain any support for Taliban (or whatever resistance that will help fighting the occupation) no matter what this might cause in a poor country like Afghanistan, white bombers shouldn't give much attention for kids or the poor who was getting their medical treatment there.

Actually this remains me of Al-Shifa plant attack in 1998, the CIA claimed that Al-Shifa, which was a national Sudanese pharmaceutical factory that provided medications for millions of Sudanese kids, was manufacturing chemical internationally banned weapons (just like it that the U.S. used against Iraqi civilians in Al-Falouja) for Osama Bin Ladin (which was too ridiculous then, even if such a developing and poor country like Sudan wants to manufacture chemical weapons, they simply can't. They're incapable).

However many human right activists warned Clinton's administration (CA henceforward) that the bombardment would probably cause a humanitarian crisis, and there was no enough intelligence information to confirm that accusation (actually, even after the bombardment, the U.S. didn't give any evidences confirm their accusations) CA didn't give a lot of attention, it is not a big deal if that would cause hundreds of thousands of kids being dead because of treatable diseases that they couldn't afford its medications.

They and their apologists regarded Africans much as we do the ants we crush while walking down a street. We are aware that it is likely to happen (if we bother to think about it), but we do not intend to kill them because they are not worthy of such consideration. - Noam Chomsky, The Most Wanted List.

Anyway, what I'm to talk about the 'politicians' does anybody know even what this world supposed to mean? What does it mean to work and gain your sustenance by being a politician? It means to know and ignore all above, everybody knows the empty argument of 'human shield' that occupiers used in Vietnam, Afghanistan, Iraq and now in Palestine to justify genocides. Everybody knows that the bombardment of Aljazira, AP and other news agencies either in Afghanistan or like what happened in the last war in Gaza, it has nothing to do with the real war, it just the endeavour to keep the information flow out, every politician knows that.

That's why it's being painful and unbearable to me to follow politics any more, I can see nothing but a world getting much worse every day and every second. Injustice, dictatorship, internationally-supported terrorism, murder, everything is in its wrong place and more and more propagandists telling people that everything is fine, we're just killing some terrorists that don't like freedom and democracy. That reminds me of one of my Dad's uneducated pastoral friends, I was setting in his car while he was listing to the radio when the presenter said something about Israel. You know most people (I can tell more than 90%) of the Egyptian countryside are stupidly anti-Semitics (due to the old anti-Israel propaganda in the 50s that used to confuse the word Zionist with Jewish) so I was expecting something like "Oh, May Allah curse the Israeli Jews" then I figured out that I have been too far for a long time when he told me, "Do you know what? We shouldn't hate Israel that much, we have made a 'peace treaty' with them" I thought for a moment he was joking until he showed me some videos for a Saudi hypocritical journalist. He was purely propagandized.

Sigh. That's all for today, I've to get back to work. Listen to this beautiful song. I hope there will be some revolutionary day which in I will sing:
